Iced Tea

Description

In those warm summer months everyone needs refreshment when they come in from the heat. What better thing to drink than Iced Tea?

This quick and easy recipe will show you how make a delicious batch of iced tea in a hurry so you quench your thirst on a humid July day!

Ingredients

  • 8 cups water, divided
  • 6 bags black tea
  • 1/3 cup sugar, optional, adjust to taste
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, bring half of the water> to a boil. Remove from the heat and add tea bags. Allow the tea bags to steep for 10 minutes.
  2. Remove the tea bags from the water. Note: If you prefer sweet tea, add the sugar or your preferred sweetener to the tea while itâ€™s still hot and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add the remaining water to the tea. Leave it refrigerated until fully chilled (about an hour).
  4. Serve with ice, lemon slices, and fresh mint if desired.
